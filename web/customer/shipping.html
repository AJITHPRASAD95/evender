Delivery

Shipping Policy

FreshLane currently provides local delivery for groceries and daily essentials from nearby approved vendors.

Serviceability

Availability depends on your saved delivery location, vendor radius, shop open status and product stock.

Delivery timeline

Most orders are estimated at 20-35 minutes where serviceable. Actual time may vary based on distance, preparation time, order size, traffic, weather or operational constraints.

Delivery charges

Delivery and platform fees are calculated at checkout before payment. The final payable amount is displayed before you proceed with Razorpay payment.

Failed delivery

If delivery cannot be completed due to an incorrect address, unavailable customer or access restrictions, support will contact you to resolve the order.
