Cancellation and Refunds Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Cancellation

Orders may be cancelled before the vendor accepts or starts preparing the order. Once preparation or dispatch starts, cancellation may not be possible for perishable or fresh items.

Refund eligibility

Refunds may be approved for failed payment, duplicate payment, unavailable products, vendor rejection, non-delivery, wrong item, missing item or quality issues reported with reasonable proof.

Refund timeline

Approved refunds are initiated through Razorpay or the original payment method. Normal refunds usually take 5-7 working days after initiation, depending on the bank/payment provider.

Partial refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for missing, rejected or unavailable items while the rest of the order is fulfilled.
