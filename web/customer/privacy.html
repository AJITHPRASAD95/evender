Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

We collect only the information needed to provide account, ordering, delivery and support services.

Information we collect

Name, phone number, email address, delivery address, location coordinates shared by you, cart details, order details and payment status information.

How we use it

We use this data to create accounts, show nearby shops, process orders, coordinate delivery, send support updates, prevent fraud and improve service quality.

Payments

Payments are handled by Razorpay. We receive payment status, order identifiers and transaction references, but we do not store sensitive payment credentials.

Sharing

Relevant order and address details may be shared with vendors and delivery partners only for fulfilment. We do not sell customer personal data.
